ChatPlus Master Controls

This is the Master Control editor for ChatPlus.

Your names

Since ChatPlus does NOT have access to any information from SOI, enter the names you use here.
Names must include the tail
Do not put spaces in names. [Bill Gates] would become billgates@soi


(If, for security reasons, you choose not to enter this information, ChatPlus will still work, but certain features may work slightly differently.)

Buddy List

The list of nicknames that are on your buddy list. Like the nickname list,
names must include the tail
Do not put spaces in names. [Bill Gates] would become billgates@soi

Rooms to never see

Any rooms listed here will never show on the new HOT list, regardless of the realm that is selected.

Host list view

Do you want to see the new HOT list with each name separated by commas as the old HOT list worked, or do you want to see each name on its own line?

Show homepage hint

In chat rooms, you may click on a name to see their home page or add them to your buddy list. To remind you, an optional dashed line may be put under the name.

Force link underlines

In some rooms, the room author has choosen to hide the underlines that are normally on links or to change the mouse pointer to a different shape.
